Our Company

Annaly Capital Management, Inc. is a leading diversified capital manager with investment strategies across mortgage finance.

A Market Leader

Proven over 20+ years to provide sustainable yield for shareholders, we strive to deliver attractive investment returns throughout market cycles. Our diversified investment strategies include agency mortgage-backed securities, mortgage servicing rights and residential real estate. With $11 billion in permanent capital(1), we are a leader across the residential mortgage finance market. Annaly has elected to be taxed as real estate investment trust (“REIT”) and is among the world’s largest REITs.

Annaly's greatest strength is our ability to leverage the power of our liquidity, scale and diversification. The combination of our business strategies is intended to enhance risk-adjusted returns over time through investment optionality and risk management.

A Culture of Excellence

Annaly brings vision, agility and rigor to the process of investment and finance. We seek to continually evolve and expand our institution through disciplined strategy to best serve our shareholders, vitalize local communitites and strengthen the economy.

Proven Leadership

The deep and varied expertise of our talented professionals has supported our successful evolution to the diversified capital manager we are today. Our leadership team brings professional experience across a variety of sectors, credit cycles, backgrounds, functions and market environments.
